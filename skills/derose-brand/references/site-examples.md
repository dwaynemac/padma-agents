# DeROSE Site Examples

## Learn DeROSE

- Fixed top navigation with strong blue brand presence.
- Editorial headings paired with practical application UI.
- Repeated horizontal sections, sliders, and curated blocks.
- Small, frequent CTAs instead of oversized hero actions.
- Best reference for dashboards, course platforms, reservation flows, and content-rich product UI.

## DeROSE Apps

- Minimal white landing page with a simple decision split.
- Centered content and clear routing into the right platform.
- Very little ornamentation beyond brand color and typography.
- Best reference for hub pages, chooser screens, and low-friction product entry points.

## DeROSE Cervino

- Local-school trust and conversion style.
- Clear top navigation, pricing cards, FAQ sections, and direct contact actions.
- Editorial framing combined with straightforward operational content.
- Concrete typography source: headings use `Sanomat Web` loaded from local `Sanomat-LightItalic-Web.woff2` and `.woff`; body defaults to `Helvetica, sans-serif`.
- Best reference for school websites, pricing pages, FAQ pages, and contact-first marketing.

## DeROSE Method

- Larger editorial marketing site with strong section-based storytelling.
- Repeated "Comienza ahora" CTA rhythm without oversized button treatment.
- Testimonials, media quotes, instructor showcases, and institutional proof blocks.
- Best reference for homepages, institutional landing pages, and narrative marketing pages.

## How To Choose

- Use Learn when the surface is product-like and operational.
- Use Apps when the goal is quick orientation and platform selection.
- Use Cervino when the page must build local trust and convert through clarity.
- Use Method when the page should feel institutional, global, and editorial.

Mix patterns only when the surface genuinely needs it. Start from the shared DeROSE rules first.
